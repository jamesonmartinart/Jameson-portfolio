About

Jameson Martin is a designer and illustrator creating visuals for the music industry and beyond. His work pairs airbrush textures with a retro-future sensibility - bold geometry, vibrant contrast, and surreal, dreamlike imagery.

With roots in portraiture and fine art, he often places the human figure inside fragmented, design-driven environments that feel like found artifacts from a proto-digital era. Influenced by vintage magazine covers, Japanese graphic design, and modern music culture, Jameson builds bold, tactile images designed to read instantly across merch, posters, album covers, and campaigns.

Selected clients: The Neighbourhood, Destroyer, Los Lobos, Jonathan Richman, FolkYEAH!!! Events, The Merch Collective, Bakery Agency
